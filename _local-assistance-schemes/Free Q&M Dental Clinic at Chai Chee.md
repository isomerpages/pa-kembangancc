<p>Residents within Kembangan-Chai Chee may receive free dental sessions
from Q&amp;M dentists at Blk 26 Chai Chee Road.
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Eligibility</strong>
</p>
<table>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Resident must be a Singaporean Citizen or Permanent Resident and reside
within Kembangan-Chai Chee</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Resident staying in Rental Blocks or Blue CHAS cardholders</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<p><strong>Benefits</strong>
</p>
<table>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Free dental session which only includes the following services:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Scaling and Polishing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Extractions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fillings</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<h4>Application</h4>
<table>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Eligible residents may call Kampong Kembangan CC at 64496022 or Kampong
Ubi CC at 67459919 to book a slot.</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
